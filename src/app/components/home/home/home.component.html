We are delighted to invite you to the wedding weekend of

Darian & Ashleigh

13 - 15 October 2023

Montpellier, Tulbagh
33°16'29.1″S 19°06'59.8″E

Details:

We have carefully planned an unforgettable weekend filled with joy, laughter, and cherished memories.

To kick off the festivities, we invite you to join us on Friday for pre-wedding drinks and casual dinner (which will be either pizza or a burger braai).
This will be the perfect opportunity to mingle with loved ones, relax, and toast to the joyous celebration ahead. Please note this will be for your own account.

On Saturday, the main event unfolds with our ceremony beginning promptly at 15h00.
Following the ceremony, a delightful reception awaits, where we will dine and dance.
Throughout the evening, a cash bar with card facilities will be available to cater to your refreshment needs.

For your convenience, accommodation is available on the farm for our guests. Considering that we have exclusive use of the venue,
we kindly request that you consider the options provided in the available spreadsheet, please see siteplan to assist you,
allowing you to fully immerse yourself in the celebration and enjoy a relaxing stay.
However, if you would prefer alternative accommodations, Tulbagh offers an abundant choice for you to choose from in close proximity.

As we want all our guests to fully enjoy the festivities, we kindly request that this be an adult-only affair.
We appreciate your understanding and hope this allows for an evening of uninhibited revelry and celebration.


There will be breakfast options available on Saturday and Sunday at R110 each.
Please note that breakfast will be charged to your own account and can be requested on the RSVP form. Some accommodation is self-catering and some includes breakfast.

The weekend will come to a close on Sunday, with check-out time at 10am.

Your presence will make our wedding day complete, we will therefore not be compiling a gift registry.
If you however wish to honour us with a small gift, cash towards our honeymoon will be gratefully accepted. EFT details are available upon request.

Please RSVP by 1 August 2023, and if you have any questions or need further assistance, do not hesitate to reach out to us.

With love and anticipation,
Darian & Ashleigh
